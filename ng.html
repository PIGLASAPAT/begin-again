SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

EAntensified Academic Excellence

Tutorial sessions will be available for students, not only during the start of classes but also before the start of the semester. These refresher sessions aim to prepare and equip them with the essential topics for their engineering and architecture subjects. By the time we go back to the face to face setting, we will also offer sessions where we can  experience the missed lab activities. Also, for smoother channeling of academic concerns and requests for tutorial sessions, each course will appoint an academic chief officer who will then be part of the Academic Team of the EASEC. To aid the students with their necessary learning materials we will set up a Google drive for students to access supplementary learning materials such as lecture notes and problem sets. 

EAxposure: Building Strong External Linkages

This year, we will be having an EAxposure program where SEA students may have an opportunity to practice their program in small and big companies in the Philippines. This aims to reinforce their learnings and skills, and prepare them for the life after Ateneo. In addition, to showcase our graduating students, during the SEA Fair we will include a career fair. Also, we will expose the students to different opportunities that will sustain them and help them grow such as through inter school competitions and external scholarship programs. 

EAthernet: A More Connected SEA Community through the SEA Website

To connect more to theTigers, we will establish the SEA website where all information regarding the EASEC will be accessible, including available external opportunities and scholarships. For inclusivity and transparency, a president’s report will be released every month to update the Tigers of what the EASEC has done for the cluster and also, in SAMAHAN. The SEA Help Desk will also be stationed in the website to cater the concerns of the students. Each victory of the Tigers will be posted on the SEA website. With EAthernet, everyone is informed and heard.
