SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

In Beginning Again as one Ateneo, we bring you a student government focused on student interests, social involvement, recovery, and information. This year, the SAMAHAN will seek for a more inclusive and lively university spirit.

Rising Ateneo

1. COVID Watch x iHELP SAMAHAN - With the country being in a national public emergency, the SAMAHAN, together with the Department of Campaigns and Advocacies and iHELP SAMAHAN will monitor the progress of the country in dealing with COVID-19. In collaboration with the NSEC and NSMSEC, this platform shall inform the student body with real-time information of government response through the official SAMAHAN Website.

2. OSF - Opening of School Facilities: We imagine a university that is pursuing a blended form of learning next academic year. With this, it is the mandate of SAMAHAN to assist students who are in need of university services and facilities for their academic needs. (e.g. gazebo areas for internet connection, drafting rooms for output-based Architecture classes and etc.)

All out Blue

1. All out Blue - In rebuilding our sports culture in the university, we will bring back activities that shall cultivate camaraderie and leadership in sports through hosting the first online palarong atenista through the celebration of athlete's week.

2. Begin Again - In collaboration with the Office of Student Affairs and AFYOP, this year opener will welcome all Ateneans and most especially our first-year students. In this event, we will introduce the different organizations, SECs, and talents of the university to everyone.

3. Ateneo Sui Generis Assembly - AdDU Sui Generis leadership has always been the hallmark leadership program of the university. With this, we will convene and host the first ever Ateneo Sui Generis Assembly by inviting Buklod Atenista ad Ateneans across the country to share their common interest in societal advocacies.

Samahan ng Pinas, SAMAHAN para sa Pinas 2022

1. Sustainable Partnership - Partnering with university offices through UCEAC and Blue Vote, and with external organizations like Rotary Davao and Sunstar Davao.

2. Lakbay Para Sa 2022 / Leadership Caravans - We will expand the dialogue to outside the university and learn the circumstance our fellow Mindanaoans face which will come from the ground itself through voter engagement.

3. DREAM PH - A pakighinabi session with the next Philippine President during the end of our term. Through this session, the AdDU community will know the plans of the next president concretely and will be a means for us to hold him/her accountable.
