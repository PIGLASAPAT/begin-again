SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

How do we re-envision?

1. Griffins, EMPOWER

Indeed, nobody was able to anticipate the sudden shift that we have undergone this year. We were caught off guard and thus, our inadequacies have become clearer to us. This is why we need to EMPOWER ourselves by filling in whatever is lacking.
·        
Borrow, Lend, and Loan A Book

In an effort to give assistance to students who are having financial difficulties during these trying times, books and other academic references will be purchased. These materials will then be made available for loan or for borrowing. Moreover, this program will facilitate an appropriate process to cater students
who are willing to lend their used books to other students.
     
The establishment of an official organization for public speakers.

The Accountancy Cluster has a lot to offer in the field of public speaking. In order to empower these potentials, an official organization composed of debaters, orators, and speakers, in general, will be established. The members of this organization will undergo regular training and will be provided with materials and other necessary resources.
·        
Scholarship Opportunities

BSA and BSMA students who are willing to start their CPA and CAT exam preparations will be given a chance to have their tuition fees or a portion of such fees, be sponsored. A rigorous selection process that will involve not only the executive council but also some of the faculty, will be conducted in order to ensure that such an opportunity will be given to deserving students.

2. Griffins, REFINE

This year we have surpassed the unfamiliar challenges that the new normal has brought upon us. We have shown that we are indeed champions who are always above par. But we cannot stop here. In order to fly higher, we have to continuously REFINE our wings by touching up the things that we already consider as our strengths. 

Webinars and tutorial sessions with notable academicians.

To give more opportunities for learning, tutorial sessions with some of the notable educators in the country will be conducted. This will serve as a reinforcement to the academic aid that is being facilitated by the regular peer to peer tutorial sessions that is specifically spearheaded by the ASSETS.

Career Path Conventions

To give our students better guidance on which path to take after graduation, we will conduct a series of career path conventions. This will help our students make a shift from the general view of our future profession to a more specific understanding of the different options that are available.
     
Official Virtual Platform

One strength that the Accountancy cluster has is the one-of-a-kind companionship that has developed among its members. For the next school year, an official virtual platform will be established where all of the students can better connect with each other. Moreover, the cluster has always taken pride in the talents that it has which is why this platform will also be utilized to facilitate The Griffins’ Week and the Gabi ng Parangal, where everyone gets a chance to showcase their potential.
