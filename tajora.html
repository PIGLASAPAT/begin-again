SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

This year, Susulong tayo, WOLVES! We will continue in Weaving Our Legacy through Visible and Empowered Student Service Under Sulong, WOLVES! are our three key platforms - Howl Up, Pack Pride, The HUMLET Website

HUMLET, it is possible to Howl Up!

This will be an avenue for students to be heard. Initiatives for all class concerns and project suggestions will be more prioritized. With the ongoing situation, a stronger information dissemination mechanism should also be implemented to ensure that all individual classes are functioning and aware of the situation inside the cluster, university and the society.

Howl Up: HUMLET Advocacy Week - Going forward, there will be a justifiable focus on public health that may overshadow the importance of languages and communication. Howl Up is an avenue for students to contribute in spreading public health information effectively to promote awareness and extend participation in student-led activities inside the cluster.

HUMLET, it is possible because we have our Pack Pride:

In the next era of leadership, we aim to acknowledge every wolf inside our cluster by recognizing the efforts they create through responsive and inclusive initiative for the pack. In the wilderness, we will value every wolf.

Our Pack Merch - Support the pack through a year-long fundraising drive for talents and athletics support. This is to provide the needed financial resources for the various council projects and efforts and to secure financial aid/funding for HUMLET Athletes and Performers.

PACK Fest - In partnership with AdDU-to-cart and Support Local Davao, a digital space for students to promote their artistry will be created. Through PACK Fest, different products and services will also be promoted in a 2-day virtual exhibit exclusive for small businesses and freelance artists inside the cluster.

Finally, HUMLET, it is possible with The HUMLET Website

Students in the cluster will have an organized system where they can see different HUMLET highlights and can easily access various updates, transparency reports, information about the cluster and a page for graduating and graduate students where they can have access to get their needed documents. In this HUMLET website we have:

WOLF GALLERY - Students can have a chance to submit their works. Different literary or artworks of the students will be showcased. Timberwolves and Wolfpack will also have a page on the website where they can promote their artistry.

HUMLET BUTTON  - This feature can be accessed in HUMLET Website. This is a downloadable guidebook and checklist document for the graduating students to help them in keeping track of their graduation requirements.

WOLF CONNECT - Stronger collaboration with AdDU Career Center and Alumni Hub to bring opportunities for our graduating students. Aside from the guidebook, a booklet will be provided to access a link of possible job opportunities they could get after their graduation.

Wolves, let us pave the way for us to weave our legacy. Let us pave the way for Sulong, WOLVES! which aims to unify every wolf in the HUMLET Community. Susulong tayo, WOLVES!
