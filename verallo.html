SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

1. Ad Hoc Committee for the Establishment of the First OVP

An ad hoc committee that will establish the very first Office of the Vice President will be created before the start of our term. Through this committee, the Internal Rules of Procedures of the Student Assembly will be formed, with an emphasis on the standards and other bases to be followed in reviewing, accepting, and/or rejecting new committees, appointments, policies, and programs made by the SAMAHAN Central Board.

The ad hoc committee will be created before the start of our term, so that appointments made by the SCB on April 01 will automatically undergo the screening process inside the Student Assembly.

2. SAMAHAN Department of Academic Affairs

A new SAMAHAN Department to be officially called as the SAMAHAN Department of Academic Affairs will be formed, in order to help the students’ difficulty with the online class setup. Similar to Accountancy Cluster’s newly launched Accountancy Students’ Scholastic Events and Tutorial Support (ASSETS), the following are the functions of this new department:

a. Conduct and regularize tutorial sessions across all clusters in Ateneo twice a month;

b. Collaborate with AdDU Peer Consulting Program so that tutor hours rendered by university scholars will be credited as community service hours;

c. Communicate with notable alumni and other professionals to invite them to discuss difficult and crucial topics over the course of the tutorial sessions;

d. Create a systematic and comprehensive Google Drive per cluster, which shall contain relevant academic materials available for sharing to all students (this is still dependent on the relative contexts of the clusters, especially to courses which do not allow the sharing of specific academic materials); and;

e. Render efforts in preparing review or training sessions for university quizzers who will participate in different academic quiz bowls.

3. SAMAHAN Department for Disaster Risk Response

We have seen the importance of iHelp SAMAHAN this year, so in our term, we will build stronger foundations and clearer goals as we expand iHelp to an ad hoc department in the SAMAHAN to be officially called as the SAMAHAN Department for Disaster Risk Response.

By having a department dedicated for this endeavour, issues on budget and systematic implementation will be resolved. Plus, there would be a dedicated group that would carry out all the programs under this new department.
