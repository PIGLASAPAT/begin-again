SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

SAMAHAN FLEX - for a more efficient and FLEXible SAMAHAN.

READY SAMAHAN 

1. SAMAHAN Blue Print 

Major Platform for: New Normal/Recovery - for Ateneo

This digital guidebook will have content such as tutorials and tips on how to navigate the new digital platform and the online university as a whole. It will also contain preparations and health protocols when the time comes for face-to-face lab classes and for students who may need access to the university for their academic needs.

2. Enhanced Website

Minor Platform for: New Normal/Recovery - for Ateneo 

With SysDev, we will upgrade the SAMAHAN Website to give the student body easy-to-access valuable information. Partnering with the Office of the President and iHelp SAMAHAN, we will provide live COVID-19 response updates on the website. 


CLOSER SAMAHAN

1. SAMAHAN Alerts

Lone Platform for: Student Body/OSG Mandate

For our term, we will make sure that all information will be received by all Ateneans. We will continue maximizing text messaging to reach the student body, with the assurance that all our social media accounts will become more active in answering the questions of the student body. 

EXPANDED SAMAHAN

SAMAHAN Safe Space

In partnership with the SS SEC and SAMASIKOFIL, we will create the SAMAHAN Safe Space and other initiatives that promote mental well-being. We will work hand-in-hand with the College Guidance Office in order to provide the student body with professional assistance.
