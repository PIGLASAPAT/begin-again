SPECIFIC PLANS OF ACTION

“Surf’s up Sharks! Big waves are coming.’’ 

1.  Experience the Stoke “Home is Where the Wave is!”

 “Experience the Stoke” is an idea that must be incorporated in all SOE activities. In the face of uncertainty brought by the pandemic, we will raise the bars for we will guarantee the “Stoke Experience” in each and every SOE experience. This year, we will strive to reinforce and innovate annual activities and create a more engaging environment for the students.

2. The Seaside “Ang Baybayin ay sa Atin”

The Seaside is a revamp of the SOE Help Desk which aims to expand its scope of assistance in terms of personal, academic, and data difficulties of the students. A designated year level inquiry peer lines will be open all the time for concerns and personal problems. We will also allot an emergency fund so that students who are in need may borrow load anytime.  

3. I Pass On Philippines #ParasaBataParasaBayan #passIon 

I Pass On Philippines is an AdDU-SOE led movement “para sa bayan at para sa bata”. The project aims to become a platform for education students to advocate for a world where every child's future is safeguarded by humanity. This initiative will be the umbrella movement for SOE activities such as (1) Public Information Campaigns via easy-to-digest fast facts and statements (2) Online Storytelling (3) Engagements and (4) Projects for a Cause.
